Frequently Asked Questions

Can I return my order?

Absolutely! We understand that sometimes items may not meet your expectations. Here's how our return process works:
If you find that your purchase isn't quite what you expected, you can initiate a return within 30 days from the date of purchase. Just ensure that the item is unused and in its original packaging. To start the return process, please contact our dedicated customer support team at Customer Support. They'll guide you through the process and provide any necessary assistance.

How long do I have to return an item?

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to initiate a return. Don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team if you need any help with the return process.

Who covers the return shipping costs?

While customers are generally responsible for return shipping costs, exceptions are made if the return is due to an error on our part or if the item is defective. If you have any questions or concerns about return shipping, please don't hesitate to contact our customer support team for clarification at Customer Support.

What happens after I initiate a return?

Once you've initiated a return, simply send the item back to us following the instructions provided by our customer support team. Once we receive the returned item and inspect it, we'll notify you via email regarding the approval or rejection of your refund. Feel free to reach out to our customer support team if you have any questions about the status of your return or refund.

Can I exchange an item?

Yes, you can exchange an item! If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, just follow our return process and then place a new order for the desired item. If you need any assistance with the exchange process, don't hesitate to contact our customer support team for help at Customer Support.

What should I do if I receive a damaged or defective item?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer support team immediately. We'll work with you to resolve the issue as quickly as possible, whether that involves providing a replacement, issuing a refund, or offering another solution. Your satisfaction is our top priority, so please don't hesitate to get in touch with us.

Are there any discounts available for bulk purchases of books or comics?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk purchases of books and comics. Whether you're a school, library, or retailer looking to buy in large quantities, we can provide competitive pricing and personalized assistance. Contact our customer support team to inquire about bulk purchase discounts and pricing.

Can I request a specific book or comic that is not currently available on your website?

Absolutely! If there's a specific book or comic you're looking for that is not currently listed on our website, please let us know. Our customer support team will do their best to source the item for you or provide recommendations for similar titles.

Are your books and comics available in digital formats?

No, at the moment, our books and comics are not available in digital formats. However, we are actively working to expand our offerings and provide digital versions compatible with major e-book readers. Stay tuned for updates on our digital availability, and thank you for your patience as we strive to enhance your reading experience.
